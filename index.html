Expert ADA Compliance Markings

Ensure accessibility and avoid costly fines with our comprehensive ADA solutions.

Navigate ADA Regulations with Confidence

The Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) has strict requirements for parking lots to ensure equal access for individuals with disabilities. Navigating these federal, state, and local codes can be complex. We remove the guesswork and guarantee your property is fully compliant.

Our ADA Compliance Services Ensure:

  • Correct Number of Accessible Stalls: Based on your total parking capacity.
  • Proper Dimensions: Correct width for parking spaces and access aisles.
  • Van-Accessible Spaces: Clearly marked and properly sized.
  • Correct Signage: Required signs at the proper height.
  • Accessible Paths: Clearly marked paths from accessible parking to the entrance.

High-Quality Pavement Sealing

Protect your asphalt investment with a rich, black finish that lasts.

Protect, Preserve, and Beautify

Asphalt is a significant investment. Pavement sealing (or sealcoating) is the most critical maintenance step you can take to protect it. Our commercial-grade sealant forms a tough, protective layer that shields your pavement from its worst enemies: water, oil, gas, and UV rays.

The Benefits of Pavement Sealing:

  • Extends Pavement Life: Prevents oxidation and water penetration, the leading causes of cracks.
  • Improves Appearance: Restores the rich, black finish of new asphalt, boosting curb appeal.
  • Resists Gas & Oil Spills: Prevents fuel and oil from softening and damaging the asphalt.
  • Saves You Money: Proactive sealing costs pennies on the dollar compared to expensive asphalt repairs or replacement.

The True Cost of Cutting Corners: Why Quality Materials Matter

Posted on July 1, 2025 by Right Choice Striping

It can be tempting to opt for the cheapest quote when you're looking to get your parking lot striped. However, when it comes to pavement markings, you truly get what you pay for. Low-quality, water-downed paint might look good for a few weeks, but it fades quickly under the sun, chips easily under traffic, and ultimately requires frequent and costly re-application.

At Right Choice Striping, we believe in doing the job right the first time. That's why we exclusively use premium, durable traffic paint from trusted manufacturers. This paint is specifically formulated to withstand heavy vehicle traffic, harsh weather conditions, and UV degradation. While the initial investment might be slightly higher, the long-term value is undeniable. Our lines stay brighter for longer, meaning your lot remains safe and professional-looking for years, not months. It's an investment in longevity, safety, and a professional image that pays for itself by reducing the need for constant touch-ups and maintenance.

The Critical Importance of ADA Compliance for Your Business

Posted on June 15, 2025 by Right Choice Striping

Ensuring your parking lot is compliant with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) is a legal requirement, but it's also much more than that. It's a statement that your business is welcoming and accessible to every member of the community. Non-compliance can lead to significant fines, lawsuits, and damage to your reputation.

The regulations can be complex, covering everything from the precise number of accessible stalls required based on your lot's total capacity, to the exact dimensions of those stalls and their access aisles, to the height and placement of signage. At Right Choice Striping, we are experts in these regulations. We take the guesswork out of compliance, ensuring every aspect of your lot meets or exceeds federal, state, and local standards. Let us help you provide safe, equal access and protect your business from liability.

How Often Should You Restripe Your Parking Lot?

Posted on June 8, 2025 by Right Choice Striping

Faded lines are more than just an aesthetic issue; they are a significant safety hazard. Worn markings can lead to parking confusion, inefficient use of space, and even accidents. So, how often should you have your lot restriped?

The answer depends on two main factors: traffic volume and climate. A high-traffic retail center will require more frequent maintenance than a small office park. Similarly, areas with harsh sun and heavy rain will see paint degrade faster. As a general rule, for most commercial lots in our region, we recommend a professional restriping every 18 to 24 months. This schedule ensures your markings remain highly visible, keeping your lot safe, organized, and looking its best. We can work with you to assess your specific needs and create a maintenance schedule that protects your property and your budget.
